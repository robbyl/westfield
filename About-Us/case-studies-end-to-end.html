Greenwoods can provide a complete package encompassing a wide range of capabilities including: custom built racks; bespoke power solutions; fibre and cable assembly; kitting; warehousing; product supply and installation. Greenwoods worked closely with Fujitsu Telecommunications Europe Limited to support their 21CN requirements and provided a complete end-to-end solution.


The Problem

Faced with the need to house and test 2048 household modems for data and voice use, as part of the testing for their 21CN kit, Fujitsu approached Greenwoods to design, build and install a bespoke solution for this requirement.

The rack requirements were so specialised there were key problems that had to be addressed during the design stage including space constraints, heat dissipation and interference. Utilising the expertise across Greenwoods, an appropriate solution was achieved in a very short time frame.


The Solution

Greenwoods provided a turnkey package to provide the customer with the required solution. A dedicated Project Management team were appointed, working closely with Fujitsu the Project Team co-ordinated the design, build and installation of the solution.

Telfab, the rack manufacturing arm of Greenwoods, designed a prototype. The custom-made side access, fan cooled lab rack utilised 2mm mild steel and incorporated a special call centre interface panel. This was then strengthened to ensure it could cope with the 500k weight of the finished rack.

Bespoke power solutions were supplied by Greenwoods company Telecom Protection Technologies Ltd (TPT). The 25 way power distribution units utilised in the rack were a combination of left and right facing, eight in total.

Greenwoods’ multi-functional in-house workshop manufactures cable assemblies, including enhanced DSL modems and custom designed interfaces.

All component parts were assembled and configured by staff in the specialist rack build facility at the Greenwoods Headquarters in Coleshill, Birmingham.

Esprit, the in-house transport company, delivered the solution in vehicles with air ride suspension and tail lifts, direct to site for installation ensuring everything met the required deadlines.

To complete the project, the solution was then installed and commissioned by Greenwoods’ engineers.


Fujitsu benefits from:

  • A complete end-to-end solution
  • Experience and expertise
  • Greater control over the project process
  • A short lead time due to easy access to component parts
