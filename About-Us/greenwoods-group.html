Telecom Protection Technologies Limited (TPT)

Telecom Protection Technology (TPT) is a manufacturer of power and protection systems for communications networks. Founded in 1993, it now operates two divisions, the cable management division and the transient over voltage and surge protection division. The cable management division designs, manufactures and supplies a range of copper and fibre cable management and interconnection products, for both internal and external applications. The transient over voltage and surge protection division specialise in providing lightning and over voltage protection systems, to the communications industry.
