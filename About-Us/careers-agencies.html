Information for Recruitment Agencies
...............................................................................................................................

Unfortunately due the proliferation of speculative calls we continue to receive on a daily basis and the need to manage our time effectively on internal HR business efficiency we will no longer be taking unannounced telephones calls from Recruitment agencies. This applies to all HR staff. Please see the attached questions and answers for information on how to provide your details.

Question: Do Greenwoods use Recruitment Agencies when recruiting for either Temporary or Permanent Staff?

Answer: Yes we do, however it does depend on the position, timescales, expertise etc. We may also use (instead of or together with an agency/ies) the jobcentre, advertise on our own website, advertise internally and advertise on job boards i.e. www.jobsite.co.uk.


Question: Do Greenwoods have a Preferred Agency Supplier List?

Answer: There is no set supplier list currently; the recruitment method and type of agency we shall use will depend on the position.


Question: How do I get my details across so that Greenwoods can use my agency?

Answer: We do not accept phone calls from speculative Recruitment agencies, instead please send the following information (below) to reception@greenwoodscomms.com and your details shall be put on file. If we are interested in using your agency then we shall call you back when such a vacancy arises.

  • Name of Agency
  • Job Area/s that you cover and what you specialise in
  • Whether you specialise in Temporary or Permanent or both
  • Percentage Rates for Permanent staff and how you work out the charge rate for temporary staff. If it’s negotiable then we shall still need to understand what the lowest rate is
  • Your method of recruitment i.e. to you meet with all of your candidates face to face, over the phone, etc


Question: What can I do if I see a position advertised on your website and feel I can assist?

Answer: Please email reception@greenwoodscomms.com confirming the above details.
