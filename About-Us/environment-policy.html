Environmental Policy
...............................................................................................................................................................................................................................
 












At Greenwoods Group Ltd (incorporating Greenwoods Communications Ltd, Greenwoods Coleshill Ltd, Rail Order Ltd & Telecom Protection Technologies Ltd) we strive for excellence in providing services and products to the Telecommunications Industry. We place a high value on minimising our impact on the environment, and are committed to the maintenance and improvement of environmental practice in our day to day operations as a telecommunications product and service provider.

In order to achieve our aims, the Chief Executive Officer, Chief Operating Officer, Company Directors and Senior Managers will at all times ensure:
  • Compliance with relevant environmental legislation, regulations and other requirements to meet environmental and customer needs.
  • Prevention of pollution through the elimination or reduction of emissions and minimising waste generation through application of reducing, reusing and recycling.
  • Continual improvement of the environmental management system.
  • Communication of this Environmental Policy internally to employees and externally as appropriate.
  • That environmental objectives and targets are set and reviewed at regular intervals.
  • To control processes so that we have a balance with the business and the Environmental aspects of our activities (Social Governance) and ensure Sustainability.
In addition, objectives for the following 12 months will address the following environmental requirements:
  • The management and control of natural resources to reduce usage.
  • The management and control of the fleet vehicle carbon emissions to reduce the rate of emission.
  • Lowering the risk of an environmental incident & ensuring that arrangements are in place to control an incident and minimise any potential impact.
  • Improving environmental communication.

I am personally committed to this Environmental Policy and with the participation of all employees we can ensure that this policy is an effective part of Greenwoods company culture.



Date: 18th January 2012

Mr W J Greenwood (Chief Executive Officer)
