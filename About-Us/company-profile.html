Greenwoods Communications is the UK's leading independent provider of multi-vendor solutions for those involved in the communications infrastructure market. The company is part of the Greenwoods Group.

The company supplies the major OEMs, network operators and mobile operators with a complete range of modular services. This includes the supply of over 20,000 products, outsourcing of procurement and logistics, custom design, manufacture, network installation and commissioning services. We work in partnership with leading industry names to deliver end-to-end business solutions.

Greenwoods Communications was founded in 1989 and as a direct result of continually satisfying our customers, the business has continued to develop. The company's operations have expanded in both scope and depth, evolving in response to the changing market. Headquarters are based in Birmingham, UK, and we now employ 350 people.

Greenwoods' main market is the UK. However, business continues to grow across Europe, the US, the Middle East and in the Far East. A large part of this is due to the company's increasing range of multi-vendor operational services, designed to meet the changing needs of our customers with the highest quality work. Installation services cover a wide range of applications based around network operators' switch and POP (Point-of-Presence) sites. Often global telecom operators will use Greenwoods' services in the UK or Europe and then request them elsewhere in the world, ensuring consistency of work.

Specialist logistics now plays an important role in our core market. Our 90,000 sqft warehouse coupled with our in-house specialist transport fleet ensures on time delivery to our customers sites. Our in-house metalwork facility is there to provide the flexibility that new telecom projects require with a quick prototype to small volume production. Partnerships with key volume suppliers are designed to provide volume production as required.
