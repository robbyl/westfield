Working at Greenwoods Communications
...............................................................................................................................

Greenwoods Communications is a leading independent telecoms company, involved in network build and ongoing operations for Europe's major carriers and OEMs. We are driven by a desire to satisfy customers and realise the importance of motivated and dedicated employees in achieving sustained customer satisfaction.

At Greenwoods, we work in an open and friendly environment and encourage people to think outside the box. We are constantly looking for new ideas and individuals who want to succeed. Our culture encourages ownership and empowers people to achieve results.

The company also realises the importance of training. Our mission is to stay at the forefront of an ever-changing market, though the delivery of new services to our customers. Staff are trained in the latest technologies and skills, to maintain our position as a leading multi-vendor independent company.

Greenwoods' philosophy on the people it employs has enabled it to become a highly successful organisation. Where vacancies exist, we welcome applications from all candidates with suitable experience, irrespective of gender, age or ethic origin.
