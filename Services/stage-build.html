Pre-Stage & Build







Greenwoods Communications operates a number of production facilities. They play a critical role in supporting our customers, which enables us to assist both network operators and OEMs, in the design and build of equipment to go directly to site.

The key emphasis of our workshops is that we insist on being able to support the customer throughout a particular requirement. The diversity of our offering makes us extremely flexible to individual needs and gives us the ability to provide custom built solutions. Managing various elements of the supply chain in-house, enables Greenwoods to be highly responsive and provide customers with a dependable single source.

Design and manufacture
We have the expertise to design racks, frames and accessories for specific requirements. This enables us to design bespoke solutions for specific project requirements. Through our machine operation, we are able to turn designs into working solutions.

Cabinet build
We are able to design and build complete networking cabinet and racking systems. We offer standard configurations, for example 19", TEP-1E and ETSI, and also work with customers to provide products that are tailored to specific environments. For example, where space is a constraint, we will design a wall mount solution or a cabinet providing increased depth.

Test and commissioning
The test and commissioning department was established to meet the growing need for multi-vendor installation and commissioning. Greenwoods has acquired the technical resources to provide this service and is conversant in the use of many OEM technologies. This enables us to test and configure vendor equipment in a controlled environment, before it is shipped to site. (...more)

Expert advice
Our experience and knowledge in this area enables us to offer advice on issues such as cabinet mounting, temperature control, fittings, cabling and installation.

Time saving
Time on-site is critical. Our workshops provide a controlled environment for preparing products, reducing required assembly time on-site.

Refurbishment
We collect site equipment for refurbishment and replace or return it, as required.

Cable assembly
We manufacture a full range of cable assemblies to support our customers. We have invested in the latest machinery to ensure minimum wastage, consistency and efficient working practices.

In-house fibre facility
The latest machinery has been added to ensure that we produce goods efficiently and of high quality. Our in-house fibre capabilities incorporate auto cable stripping equipment, insertion and return loss testing, microscopes, the latest specification interferometer equipment and stringent quality control.
