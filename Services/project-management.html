Project Management













Greenwoods Communications has gained extensive expertise in the field of network installations, with over a decade's experience in working with carriers across the globe.

Our installations operation is tightly co-ordinated by an experienced project management group. This team understands the factors that are important to the customers they deal with - namely a cost effective service of the highest quality, delivered within set time scales. Many of our project managers have joined us from carriers or OEMs, where they have past experience in similar roles. Our expertise in project management means that you can feel confident in handing over turnkey projects to Greenwoods. From sourcing required materials for an installation project to a total turnkey service, Greenwoods can co-ordinate entire network build projects.

Our project management services cover materials sourcing, installation of copper / fibre / data networks, site design, construction, mechanical and electrical building services (e.g. power, air conditioning), base station build and roll-out, logistics, network implementation, de-installation and network rationalisation. We also have a great deal of experience in managing OEM equipment as it is supplied into the network. You can turn to Greenwoods for a service as extensive as you require and can concentrate your efforts on other areas of your business in the knowledge that we will be concentrating our efforts on meeting your needs.

Project support

Our project support team is dedicated to ensuring projects run smoothly and on time. Covering activities such as site surveys, site supervision, clerk-of-works activity and site audits, they provide an additional resource to the project management team. Although the project support and project management teams work closely on a day-to-day basis, project support is entirely separate and is primarily concerned with problem prevention. If a problem is identified during a network installation project, this team is dedicated to resolving it as quickly as possible. Having no other commitments means that they will react immediately.

To ensure site work is as comprehensive as possible, we cover the following considerations:

  • Method statements
  • Health and safety procedures
  • Work permits
  • Site access
  • Supply required materials
  • Transport of equipment and materials
  • Registration with Network Management Centres
  • Test and commissioning
  • Certification - DC/AC, test certificates for fibre, cable, verification of testing, as-built information
  • Site clearance
