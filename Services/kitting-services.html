Kitting Services







Greenwoods believes that by providing job-specific installation kits, developed with our customers, that valuable time on site can be reduced. By having the exact site-specific materials available the engineer can focus on providing an on-time quality installation. Delays are therefore reduced and high volume projects can be kept on time and within budget. There is a high degree of intelligence that is required to develop and assemble these kits to assist the engineers on site. Delivery of the kits to site is also included in our turnkey capabilities.


Benefits of our Kitting services:

  • Save time on site with pre-assembled kits
  • Avoid material shortages with precise materials supplied
  • Technical and complex kits can be assembled
  • Kits can be delivered to precise requirements
