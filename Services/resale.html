Resale








Greenwoods will re-market, globally, identified surplus equipment under a revenue sharing model. This includes:

  • Storage
  • Sales
  • Supplier shipping
  • RMA processing
  • Re-packaging

Greenwoods can then generate a report every month detailing:

  • Equipment received
  • Equipment sold
  • Current stock holding
  • The appropriate purchase order to allow immediate invoicing for revenue returns to you the customer

A typical reverse logistics project incorporating resale shows a breakdown and revenue return of:

  • 35% via targeted sales
  • 35% via bulk sales
  • 25% through recycling materials break down
  • 5% of material with no value to the project

Multi-vendor equipment identified for resale is marketed real time to over 3,000 telecom brokers as well as other multiple resale disposal channels including, carriers, OEM’s, CM’s, repair houses and system integrators to realise a profitable and on-going revenue stream to develop from redundant assets and legacy “20C” technology.
