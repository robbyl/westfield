Power Services













Today more than ever, the integrity of power supply and service availability is a prime factor in the success of all organizations that are customer facing. Greenwoods Power Services and its engineers are BS7671 and NICEIC approved. We have strong technical capabilities and capacity from AC circuit provisioning and upgrading, through to detail experience and expertise in AC and DC systems with high energy storage battery back up. From a simple circuit connection / upgrade through to a delivered turnkey solution Greenwoods have the skills, products and experience to deliver to your scope and operational requirements.

Power Services

  • Supply and installation of AC & DC power schemes - 17th Edition qualified
  • DC power system maintenance & installations
  • UPS installation & AC provision / enablement works
  • UPS & battery in-life maintenance (battery discharge testing & health checks)
  • Project & Programme Management
  • 24/7 Capability - fault finding & emergency “keep alive” & re-instatement
  • Street Cabinet – Installation & Commissioning (Electrical AC & DC – active comms kit)
  • Temporary Event Installations
