Streetside Installations













Greenwoods currently undertakes street cabinet work for a number of network operators who require cross-connect, CATV connections and local loop connections in connecting new customers and making ongoing changes and network reconfigurations.

  • Cabinet design and manufacture
  • Cabinet configuration in our controlled test and build centre
    • DC power installation with battery backup
    • Primary wiring
    • Cross-connects - subscriber connections
    • Installation of multiplex equipment
    • Design and development of heat management systems, air conditioning, power, weather protection IP rating and noise emission
  • Project management -site survey, obtaining access permission from local authority, arranging site closure, planning, safety, liasing with electricity board
  • Power engineers available to connect power requirements
  • Ground preparation and installation of cabinet at local loop
  • Installation - equipment installed, terminate subscribers
  • Commissioning - test and sign off the loop
