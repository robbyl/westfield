Managed Services



Greenwoods Communications provides a range of managed services, focused on one primary objective, lowering costs for our customers. Our many years of experience in managing materials, logistics and operational network services mean that we are highly skilled in these areas. These functions are core business to Greenwoods: these are what we use to differentiate ourselves and how we add value to our products and services.

However, more commonly they are not core to our customers. Network operators want to focus on delivery of services, customer care and marketing activities to differentiate their own proposition. The cost and time involved in managing network build related functions, is increasingly being passed to specialist third parties.

Our aim is to take problems away from our customers, enabling them to concentrate on their core business, namely the delivery of service. Greenwoods works with clients to help them reduce overheads and gain a competitive lead. By choosing Greenwoods, you can feel assured that you are in safe hands and that you will always be our number one priority.
