Materials Management







The procurement, storage and logistics involved in materials can be extremely costly for network operators. Materials can also become redundant losing money and costing more to continually store. Our customers have seen major advantages in using Greenwoods to manage their inventory, realising that this is a non-core business function and an area which can be run far more efficiently by a third party.

Greenwoods Communications has gained substantial experience in materials management operations. We have the expertise to manage the entire procurement process.

  • Source specified materials
  • Work with your existing supply chain
  • Manage inventory
  • Remove obsolete stock cost effectively
  • Work with vendors to control OEM supplied equipment
  • Provide warehousing
  • Timed deliveries to specified sites
  • Report on product usage, stock levels, forecasts and deliveries


Materials management can reduce costs in the following ways:

  • Eliminates the need for warehousing
  • Reduces required stores personnel
  • Reduces the need for day-to-day procurement
  • Eliminates the cost of slow moving, redundant or damaged stock
  • Saves your time and resources in quality issues - Greenwoods investigates and reports on any problems
  • Our bulk buying enables economies of scale, which can translate into cost savings for customers
