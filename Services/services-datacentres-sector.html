Data Centres
 




Greenwoods Communications offers a range of solutions for the Data Centres market. By combining our expertise with a comprehensive portfolio of related products and services we are able to deliver quality solutions for all your Data Centre requirements.

The following list is a summary of our portfolio of products and services.


 Data Centre Services

  • Site Surveys & Floor Layout
    Having problems deciding which is the best layout for your facility? Why not take advantage of our site survey team who will advise you on how to get the best from your facility. They can also advise on the use of different methods of cable containment and best practice for air outlets in the floors.

  • Project Management Expertise
    Do you know what you require but want someone else to project manage the installation for you? We have a large team of project managers that can run the whole installation or just look after the parts you would rather a specialist team look after on your behalf.

  • Infrastructure Design & Build
    Services include raised flooring, directional air ducting, copper & fibre cable containment, and
    Hot Aisle/Cold Aisle cooling solutions.

  • Pre-Install & Post-Install Health Check
    Health checks can be arranged and can include locating high temperature spots within the existing facility.


Data Centre Products

  • Server and Cabling Racks Supply and Placement
    A wide variety of racks are available covering all eventualities from simple telecom racks through entry level server and cabling racks to high strength aluminium framed products.

  • AC/DC Power Products including UPS expertise
    Any size of system can be supplied with battery or generator backup to cater for varying stand up time requirements.

  • Intelligent Power Monitoring & Switching of power strips
    A variety of intelligent power strips are available starting with the simple Link Box version which allows a power monitor to be inserted between the supply socket and the existing power strip to monitor the Amps, Volts, Kwh, BTUs, Kg of Carbon and various other items while passing the power through the chip in the electronics to monitor the true power factor.

  • Hi-capacity Cooling Systems
    If you have hot spots or high waste heat racks or cabinets in your facility then we can supply in row or rear door fitment coolers that can each remove 35kw of waste heat or even more in certain circumstances.

  • Free Cooling Systems
    Using one of our approved sub-contract partners we can offer 'free cooling' systems to cover any capacity. The effect and benefit is that this can cut the cooling costs dramatically within a data centre. Due to annual ambient temperatures more than 80 days a year can be utilised to assist in free cooling systems and then only partial refrigeration systems may be required.

  • Cabling Systems
    We can arrange to supply and fit most cabling systems and can advise on the best for your particular requirement.

  • Cable Sealing Systems and Blanking Panels providing excellent air flow management
     To keep running costs to a minimum and keep servers running as cool and efficiently as possible all apertures including unused 19” spaces should be blanked off, to do this we offer a variety of 19” blanking plates combined with a selection of cable entry methods to stop cold air escaping, and to channel it in the correct direction.

  • Optical Distribution Frames (ODFs) and Fibre Panels
    Greenwoods manufacture, stock and supply the most comprehensive range of optical distribution hardware to ensure your demand for port density, reliability, scalability, and service throughout product lifecycle can be met.

  • Fibre Connectivity
    Greenwoods understand that connectivity is the critical link within your network, which is why we only supply ‘carrier class’ connectivity solutions that are proven to reduce common circuit risk issues and promote ‘uptime’ within the data centre.     Greenwoods pre-terminated connectivity solutions exceed the challenge of increasing connector density, physical protection, low circuit risk and speed of deployment.

  • Fibre Containment Systems
    Purpose fibre containment systems can be run overhead or under raised floors to provide routing, segregation, bend radii management and physical protection of fibre cables. Containment systems are designed to allow easy adds/changes (to cable routing), thus accommodate changes and expansions of rack/floor layouts.

  • Cable Baskets and Cable Ladders
    A variety of trays, baskets and ducts are available to address all eventualities and can be combined with cable mats where required.

  • Cable Management, Test Equipment and Consumables
    A wide range of accessories are available to cover most requirements....(more).
