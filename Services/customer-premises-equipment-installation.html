Customer Premises Equipment Installation













Satisfying customers is our number one goal. Likewise, the carriers we work for consider their customers to be their primary concern. Satisfaction is based on excellence of service, reliability and technical expertise.

Customer premises network installation is a priority as network operators look to maximise their existing infrastructure. Networks are in place and filling capacity with customer traffic is essential in generating revenue.

Our service includes the project management and the installation of customer premises network equipment and integration onto the carrier's network. Using Greenwoods enables operators to streamline their own customer network installations operation and switch on the revenue stream quickly.

Greenwoods is able to provide:

  • Full customer premises installation package - one invoice at end of project
  • Arrangement and supply of required materials
  • Labour provision
  • 24/7 fault call out
  • Fault diagnosis
  • Spares stock of network cards
  • Network architecture design
  • Method statements
  • Risk assessments for each site
  • Test and commissioning expertise
  • Project management
  • Logistical support
  • Health and safety knowledge - working at heights, confined spaces, etc.
