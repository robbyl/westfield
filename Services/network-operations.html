Network Operations













All network operators, both fixed and mobile, require an ongoing service of network operations. This consists of various activities, for example: implementation of new customer circuits, alterations to circuits, terminations and live traffic outages. Traditionally, carriers have chosen to run this service in-house. However, Greenwoods has the capability and proven track record to successfully undertake network operations on behalf of telco's, delivering cost savings.

Greenwoods has over 70 engineers deployed across the UK specifically dedicated to network operations. They are available 24/7 and specialise in reacting quickly to customer needs. Our engineers currently carry out over 10,000 physical network operation tasks per month, such as installing circuits, testing, card infills and circuit termination. All tasks are accurately recorded and itemised billing is produced. Past audits carried out by one major operator have demonstrated that Greenwoods has consistently achieved a success rate of over 99%.

Our network operations service offers:

  • Building of new circuits
  • Test and commissioning of circuits
  • Re-routing existing traffic carrying circuits - live traffic outages completed at any time of day, at pre-arranged times, ensuring minimal disruption to customers and network
  • Circuit cancellations
  • Physical connections and software implementation at each required network site
  • Addition of new network cards / swap existing cards
  • Assistance with revisions of existing network
  • Provision of technical support
  • Network migration support
  • Scheduling all work and required staff
  • Supervision of third parties working on network sites
  • Integration and updating of customer databases
Traffic Migration

Greenwoods has experience in both the co-ordination and completion of large scale migration and business critical projects. We have the expertise to advise, co-ordinate, project manage, provide technical support and oversee projects from conception to final completion. We have completed numerous large scale migration projects for major network operators. Examples of projects include:
  • PDH-SDH migration - routing high order PDH circuits, from existing PDH network to new SDH network. This enables additional network protection, improved flexibility, removal of unsupported legacy equipment and provision of additional space on-site for further expansion
  • PDH-SDH decommissioning - decommissioning and removal of PDH equipment and racks from live equipment rooms, to enable further expansion of the network
  • ATM / frame relay - installation and testing of circuits to complete connections and enable full interconnection of network elements
  • De-installation - investigation and confirmation of circuits carrying traffic and removal of redundant circuits to free up network. Recovery of equipment from customer sites for re-use
  • Frame relay passport migration - replacement of various types of cards no longer supported by OEM within frame relay passport equipment. Cards replaced carrying live traffic, in some cases requiring new cabling. Card replacement completed within agreed timescales to reduce disruption to customers and network
