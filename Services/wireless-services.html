Wireless Services













Greenwoods has gained great expertise in the mobile networks market, supplying the major mobile operators with a wide range of products and services and is now working on the next generation of mobile network technology. Our wireless installation services are designed to assist mobile operators with tight deployment requirements.

We are able to provide the following range of services for the wireless market:

  • Project management
  • Site surveys
  • Mobile base stations - design and configuration of cabinets including:
    • Thermal management - air conditioning
    • Noise emissions
    • AC/DC power
    • Signal cabling
    • Alarm functions
    • Installation of multiplex equipment
    • Commissioning
  • Network upgrades, de-installation and redeployment of equipment
  • Transmission network design / planning
  • Pre-staging
  • Installation and commissioning of radio
  • Temporary tower employment
  • Full site deployment and integration
  • Traditional equipment cabin - provide cabin and fit-out equipment
  • Flat pack equipment cabins - provide and assemble
  • Internal communications room - design and installation of internal room network architecture and electrical / mechanical requirements
  • Logistics support - co-ordination and delivery of cabins
  • Ground clearance and maintenance of base station / internal sites
  • Fabrication, interface steelwork, tower strengthening and site extension

Rigging Services

  • BTS Installation & Commissioning
  • Microwave links - installation, configuration and line of sight
  • Lightning protection - installation of lightning protection equipment
  • Antenna installation - e.g. UHF, microwave, cellular, DAB Radio
  • Rooftop steel fabrications, tower erection, edge protection and earthing
  • Optimisation and fault finding
  • Decommissioning - full site / I&C
