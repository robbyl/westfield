Fibre Services













We have invested heavily in a fibre optics facility at our premises in Birmingham and have acquired highly skilled staff in both fibre products and fibre network installation. Our engineers are trained to the highest standards and our working practices include BS 7718 (installation of fibre optic cabling) and BS EN 50173 (information technology - generic cabling systems).

Fibre Installation Services

Greenwoods provides complete project management, installation and test solutions to meet demand for fibre networks to support telecom and data infrastructures such as fast gigabit Ethernet, ATM, DWDM, SONNET, SDH, ADSL and VoIP.

  • Site survey and specification of products and services
  • Fibre network design including loss budget assessment
  • Project management of fibre installation
  • Engineers trained in Greenwoods' replica switch room to highest standards and City & Guilds approved
  • Fusion splicing / ILM (Insertion Loss Measurement) testing / OTDR (Optical Time Domain Reflectometer) testing
  • LAN/WAN premises cabling
  • Installation and termination of external fibre optic networks
  • Maintenance of existing internal and external fibre networks, 24 x 7 x 365
  • Fault finding / diagnosis
  • Polarisation Mode Dispersion (PMD) and Chromatic Dispersion testing - provide full fingerprint of the fibre optic infrastructure

In-house Fibre Facility

The latest machinery has been added to ensure that we produce goods efficiently and of high quality. Our in-house fibre cabling capabilities incorporate:

  • Auto cable stripping equipment
  • Insertion and return loss testing
  • Microscopes
  • Latest specification interferometer equipment
  • Stringent quality control - all individual fibre assemblies tested and logged
