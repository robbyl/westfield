Recovery Services








Greenwoods offer a range of recovery services for communications equipment to provide customers with the opportunity to re-capture value and properly dispose of all redundant material. These services include;

  • Decommission
  • Deinstall
  • Transport
  • Store
  • Resell
  • Recycle

The Greenwoods telecom recovery business manages projects for global operators, OEMs, other licensed operators, enterprise customers and leading industry suppliers. Unwanted and aged hardware recoveries in the UK and internationally include:

  • Multi-vendor transmission
  • Central office switching
  • Access
  • Wireless
  • CPE
  • Broadcast communications equipment

Recovery operations are certified and governed by the highest industry standards. Established procedures and documentation is deployed in the dismantling, handling, packaging, transporting and storage of materials, thereby minimising disruption to operational environments and maximising the value of recovered equipment. Advisory services include:

  • Site assessment
  • Inventory sorting
  • Auditing
  • Asset valuation
  • Project timescales
  • Post recovery support

Implementation of a recovery project offers:

  • Immediately reduced footprints at multiple sites
  • Freeing up of space in centralised warehousing
  • Reduced energy consumption
  • Associated OPEX savings

In addition, these activities provide comprehensive visibility of surplus inventory, control of these assets and a strategic approach towards network lifecycle management, recycle and resale leading to a positive impact both from a financial aspect and in the area of corporate environmental concerns.
