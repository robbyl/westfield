Structured Cabling













The high-speed transfer of data around buildings, through cities, across countries and around the world is now of vital importance to businesses. Data traffic is rapidly overtaking voice traffic, both on fixed and wireless networks. A structured cabling system provides via copper and / or fibre cabling infrastructure a network capable of carrying voice, data and video traffic around a business premises. It is a critical investment both in the short-term and to support a company's long-term goals. Not every new technology will be of benefit to all companies and it is important to choose a technology that will meet the requirements of your business today and the foreseeable future.

Greenwoods Communications is able to act as data installation partners, assisting in the design of networks and the choice of technologies, providing:

  • Design, consultancy and installation of enterprise and core networks
  • Ability to troubleshoot problems on existing network
  • Installation of Category 5 / 5e / 6 / 7 and fibre networks to cater for today's technologies such as Gigabit Ethernet
  • Implementation of high speed LAN / WAN environments
  • Wireless networking
  • Solutions tailor-made for business critical networks
  • Vendor independent solutions - structured cabling and network hardware chosen to suit individual customer projects
  • Large stockholding and logistical support
  • Wide experience of various active equipment, including Avaya, Cisco, HP and Juniper Networks

Greenwoods Communications offers the following data network installation solutions:

Consultancy
We work with clients to understand business objectives and propose IT network solutions geared to fit the business direction of our customers. We have the ability to provide expert technical help and can advise on future technologies and the best solutions for individual client needs.

Surveys
Greenwoods has the expertise to conduct structured cabling surveys on a multitude of sites, whether new Greenfield sites or in existing buildings. We work with our clients during site surveys in order to understand specific requirements prior to producing system designs.

Network Design and Structured Cabling
Experienced in-house network and cabling designers are employed to use the latest technologies such as CAD, for network design and to provide expertise in all cabling practices and standards.

Roll-out Projects
Greenwoods has the capability to provide a total turnkey installation service to implement a complete LAN / WAN environment. Project management is at the heart of this service and we are skilled in working to strict timescales and within budget. Our qualified UK engineering coverage means we can provide a flexible service and can work on various sites in parallel.

Comprehensive Support Services
Where required, we will work with a client's IT department to plan and execute moves and changes to voice and data connections around an office environment.

Network Health Checks and Audits
A network health check service is available, using the latest diagnostic equipment to ensure your network is performing as expected. Checks can be performed for network bottlenecks and errors and to validate existing cabling systems. We can also check Category 5e and 6 system conformance to ensure that they are performing to international standards and can perform tests to ensure your network will support Gigabit Ethernet applications.

Documentation
Following completion of installations, full documentation packs are prepared detailing outlet locations, cable runs, test results, warranty information, patching schedules and cabinet layouts.

Maintenance and Lifecycle Support
We provide maintenance with guaranteed response times and our wide engineering coverage enables us to react quickly to sudden network problems. Where required, Greenwoods can provide continuous backup to ensure your business critical systems perform without problems.
