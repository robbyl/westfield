Recycling Services








All Greenwoods Communications processes are:

  • Fully compliant with the WEEE (Waste Electrical & Electronic Equipment) directive and carried out in accordance with the Environmental Protection Act 1990
  • 100% regulatory compliant & meet relevant government guidelines and industry codes of practice
  • Designed to ensure environmental factors are considered during project planning and implementation
  • Continually reducing the environmental impacts and conserve natural resources
  • Allowing obsolete equipment to be returned back to original raw materials delivering minimal waste

Our expertise and resources allow us to recover and recycle telecom material from:

  • Complete national communication networks
  • Individual exchanges
  • Data centres
  • Enterprise customer premises equipment

This experience has allowed us to achieve a level of 100% recycling in respect to cables which are carefully recycled by granulation. Greenwoods are also proud to be able to offer recycling levels at over 96% on complete site recoveries. We recycle as much as physically possible including any packaging materials. All toxic and harmful materials are guaranteed to be recycled safely in accordance with current legislation.

Our recycling processes are constantly monitored and reviewed through our ISO 14001 environmental management system. This guarantees a full audit trail from collection to disposal.
