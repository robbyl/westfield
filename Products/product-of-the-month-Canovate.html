Canovate Data & Server Cabinets

Server Cabinets

Specifically designed to house servers manufactured by most of the industries leading OEMs such as, HP, Sun, Compaq and IBM.

Features:

  • Aluminium frame structure
  • Unique honeycomb structure pattern on front and rear doors, with a top cover which provides a high degree of free air ratio
  • Standard perforated front and rear doors with multipoint locking system
  • Side panels with two cylindrical locks and latches
  • Internal top sheet of 19 inch mounting rails
  • Bottom cover with three cable entrances
  • Adjustable feet
  • RAL9005
  • Perfect for data centre applications
