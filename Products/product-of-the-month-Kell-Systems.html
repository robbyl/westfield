Kell Office Server Cabinets

The Kell Office Server Cabinets are ideal for use in offices of all sizes, from small regional offices to enterprise headquarters.

Small Office LANs

The Kell Systems enclosure takes the place of an entire computer room. A single Cabinet provides thermal support for up to nine servers, plus switches, patch panels, UPS, KVM, tape drive and other peripherals. It also reduces server noise to no more than a slight background hum, similar to the noise generated by most office air conditioning systems.

Large Office LANs

The Kell Systems enclosure replaces wiring closets. Modern switches (VoIP / PoE devices) can be as hot and as noisy in operation as servers, and conventional wiring closets often do not have the cooling or soundproofing to house them. The PSE offers more than enough of both. They are designed for management of hundreds of structured cables and blend into any office environment.
