Greenwoods add Kell Office Server Cabinets to product range

Greenwoods Communications are pleased to introduce a NEW range of enclosures to its already vast range of products.

Kell Office Server Cabinets house computer servers and network equipment within offices or other environments. These enclosures remove the need for server rooms, cutting capital costs by up to 80%. Their integral cooling save kilowatts of air conditioning, reducing running costs by up to 98% and improving system reliability. Kell Office Server Cabinets are stylish, high quality furniture, lockable for security and fitted with wheels for portability and installation is quick and simple.

Business Benefits

• Replaces the traditional computer room
• Eliminates building cost and saves floor space
• Saves energy cost of computer room air conditioning
• Reduces carbon footprint
• Moves with you when you relocate to new premises
• Deploys in weeks rather than months
• Minimises IT costs through remote management

Greenwoods Communications is one of the UK's leading independent providers of custom-built solutions for telecom network operators and OEMs. The company supplies the major OEMs, network operators, mobile operators and cable TV companies with a complete range of modular services, including outsourcing of procurement and logistics, network installation and de-installation, and custom design and manufacture. Kell Systems has pioneered the design and manufacture of Portable Server Environments (PSEs), the first complete solution for deployment of servers and network hardware directly in the office workplace.
