Greenwoods awarded UK Partner of the Year by C&W Worldwide

Greenwoods Communications is proud to announce that it has been awarded UK Partner of the Year 2010 by Cable&Wireless Worldwide.

This prestigious award was presented to John Greenwood at the annual C&W Worldwide Partner Event, held in London on 6th July. The award is in recognition of the ongoing commitment of Greenwoods towards its partnership with C&W Worldwide and the achievements made in challenging times.

Greenwoods have been supplying materials and labour to C&W since the early days of deregulation in the telecoms market. Throughout that time, Greenwoods has not only introduced significant cost savings across a multitude of C&W projects, but has also challenged and adapted existing C&W operational processes to achieve greater efficiencies.

Greenwoods has also supplied C&W with racks and fibre products manufactured using its own production facilities. Collaboration between both companies has seen successful product innovations, such as the compact and versatile High Density Digital Distribution Frame. This product delivered significant cost savings to C&W, through reduced space requirements and more efficient installations. Further cost savings were achieved through the outsourcing of C&W's workshop facilities and field operations.

After more than 20 years of working together, the partnership between Greenwoods and C&W is as strong as ever, with both playing an integral part in each other's business.
