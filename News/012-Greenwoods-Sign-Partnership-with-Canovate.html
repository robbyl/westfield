Greenwoods sign partnership with Canovate for enclosure solutions

Greenwoods Communications are proud to announce its latest partnership with a top European rack and frame manufacturer.

Greenwoods is well known in the industry for its capabilities in providing bespoke enclosure solutions, but have recognised for some time the transformation within the industry, moving ever more rapidly into the enterprise market.

After many extensive 'make' or 'buy' engineering evaluations, Greenwoods have found an enclosure product range which truly meets the demanding requirements of the data centre market. Greenwoods are pleased to announce the launch of the Greenwoods / Canovate partnership to provide the industry with a true industry leading range of floor standing and wall mounted enclosures, together with a wide range of accessories to complement their existing product portfolio.

This new range of products includes; Network Enclosures, constructed with an aluminium frame having both strength and overall floor loading, Server Racks with 80% open area air flow doors available, compatible with multiple configurations (HP, Dell, IBM etc) and Wall Mounted 19 inch enclosures for SOHO, and network applications.

There is a true synergy between Greenwoods Manufacturing and Canovate which will satisfy the demands of both the telecommunications and enterprise markets.
