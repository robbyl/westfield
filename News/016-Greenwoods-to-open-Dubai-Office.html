Greenwoods to open new Dubai office

Greenwoods Communications is pleased to announce the imminent opening of its new office and warehouse in the Middle East.

Francois Bodart, International Sales Director, explained the reasons behind the new office.

"We have experienced tremendous demand and growth for our services as well as our carrier grade products by the leading operators, OEM’s and the Oil & Gas industry in this region, so in order to better serve our customers, we will be opening a facility in the centrally located hub of Dubai which will enable us to service the entire Gulf region. Our customers have welcomed this move as they would like to enjoy the same level of quality service as they have been accustomed to in the UK and Europe."

Greenwoods will be announcing further details about the new office shortly.


About Greenwoods Communications
..................................................................

Greenwoods Communications is one of the UK's leading independent providers of custom-built solutions for telecom network operators and OEMs. The company supplies the major OEMs, network operators and wireless/mobile operators with a complete range of modular services, including outsourcing of procurement and logistics, network installation and de-installation, and custom design and manufacture. Customers include Ericsson, BT, Huawei, Alcatel-Lucent, Cable&Wireless, Colt, Ciena, Fujitsu, Reliance Globalcom, Global Crossing, Nokia-Siemens Networks, Virgin Media, Everything Everywhere, O2, Vodafone. Greenwoods is actively involved in building fixed line and wireless networks throughout the UK and currently employs 350 staff.
