Greenwoods secure deal with Viking Connectors UK

Greenwoods are pleased to announce the acquisition and investment in Viking Connectors UK

This move ensures the supply of Viking BT226 connectors to the communications market, by offering a secure and flexible UK manufacturing base in Coleshill, Birmingham.

Furthermore it also allows Greenwoods to accommodate the fluctuations and urgencies of a volatile business sector by ensuring quick deliveries from stock and faster manufacturing times to meet increasingly aggressive project timescales.

Greenwoods will also be offering a quick turnaround custom cable assembly service to enhance its connector product offering.

If you would like to know more about the Viking range of interconnect solutions or indeed Greenwoods Communications' other products/services please visit www.greenwoodscomms.com or ring +44 (0)845 226 2327.
